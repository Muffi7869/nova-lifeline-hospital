About Nova Lifeline

Serving Bardoli and South Gujarat for over 16 years

Nova Lifeline Super Speciality Hospital has been serving the people of Bardoli and South Gujarat for over 16 years. What began as Lifeline Hospital under Dr. Shabbir H. Rajkotwala has grown into a full super-speciality centre — bringing world-class medical expertise to your doorstep. No need to travel to the city — everything is here.

✦ A Message From Our Founder

Our Vision for Healthcare in Bardoli

For over 16 years, my mission has been simple — to ensure that no patient from Bardoli or South Gujarat has to travel far for quality medical care. Nova Lifeline was built on the belief that advanced super-speciality treatment should be accessible to everyone, right here in their own city. Today, with a team of 31 dedicated specialists and world-class facilities, we stand by that promise every single day.

— Dr. Shabbir H. Rajkotwala, MD Medicine

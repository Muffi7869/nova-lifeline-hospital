Our Story

A Hospital Built
on a Promise.

What began as Lifeline Hospital under Dr. Shabbir H. Rajkotwala has grown into Nova Lifeline Super Speciality Hospital — a comprehensive centre bringing together the finest specialists across 27 disciplines. Our mission has always been simple: ensure that no patient in Bardoli or South Gujarat has to travel to a distant city for quality care.

Today, with 17 dedicated specialists, a 10-bed Level-3 ICU, Class 100 operation theatres, and a full diagnostic wing, we deliver on that promise every single day.

      Infrastructure

      Built for Modern Medicine

      Every department at Nova Lifeline is equipped with the technology and environment required to deliver outcomes that match the best hospitals in any major city.

      10-Bed Level-3 ICU
      Advanced critical care unit with continuous monitoring and intensivist coverage around the clock.
      Class 100 Modular OT
      International standard operation theatres with laminar airflow systems for sterile surgical environments.
      CT Scan and Imaging
      High-resolution CT scanning alongside digital X-ray and sonography for precise diagnostic imaging.
      Pathology Laboratory
      Comprehensive in-house laboratory for all routine and specialised blood and diagnostic tests.
      Dialysis Centre
      Full nephrocare unit offering scheduled and emergency dialysis sessions for renal patients.
      24-Hour Pharmacy
      On-site pharmacy stocked with all essential and speciality medications, open around the clock.

      Our commitment has always been to the patient first.
